A Holistic School Experience

At Bidii High School, we believe education extends beyond the classroom. Our vibrant student life program combines academic excellence with character development, leadership opportunities, and diverse extracurricular activities that prepare students for success in university and beyond.

From competitive sports teams to cultural clubs, debate societies to music groups, every student finds their passion and builds lifelong friendships. Our boarding and day scholar programs create a supportive community where students thrive academically, socially, and personally.

Choir, orchestra, traditional dance groups, drama club, and instrumental ensembles. Students perform at school events, festivals, and national competitions throughout the year.

Academic Societies

Leadership & Service

Sports & Athletics

Track & Field

Our athletics team competes at regional and national levels, with training in sprints, long distance, hurdles, javelin, and field events.

Basketball

Both boys' and girls' basketball teams train regularly and participate in inter-school tournaments across Nairobi and Kenya.

Football & Rugby

Our football and rugby teams have proud traditions of excellence, competing in regional leagues and national championships.

Volleyball & Netball

Students develop teamwork and athletic skills through competitive volleyball and netball programs with experienced coaches.

Student Facilities

Boarding Houses

Modern, comfortable dormitories with dedicated houseparents providing a safe home away from home for boarding students.

Dining Hall

Spacious dining facility serving nutritious, balanced meals prepared by professional chefs to keep students healthy and energized.

School Library

Comprehensive library with over 20,000 books, digital resources, and quiet study areas supporting academic excellence.

Sports Facilities

Well-maintained sports grounds, courts, and multi-purpose hall for athletics, games, and recreational activities.

Student Support Services

Health & Wellness

On-campus medical center staffed with qualified nurses providing 24/7 healthcare, first aid, and wellness programs to ensure student wellbeing.
