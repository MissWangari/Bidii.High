Annual Science and Innovation Fair 2026

Join us for Bidii High School's premier scientific event of the year! The Annual Science and Innovation Fair brings together the brightest minds from Forms 1 through 4 to showcase cutting-edge research projects, innovative experiments, and technological breakthroughs. This year's theme, "Science for Sustainable Development," aligns with Kenya's Vision 2030 and emphasizes practical solutions to real-world challenges.

Students will present projects across various disciplines including Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Computer Science, Mathematics, and Environmental Science. The fair provides a platform for young scientists to demonstrate their research skills, critical thinking, and creativity while competing for prestigious awards and scholarships. Parents, educators, and industry professionals are invited to witness the next generation of Kenyan innovators.

Event Highlights

  • Over 50 student projects competing across six science categories
  • Keynote address by Prof. Jane Mwangi, Kenyan Environmental Scientist
  • Robotics and coding demonstrations by our ICT club
  • Interactive science experiments and live demonstrations
  • Awards ceremony with scholarships worth KES 200,000
