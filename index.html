Our Story

Educating Minds, Inspiring Hearts

We create a learning environment that goes beyond mere academic instruction to foster a, passion for learning, critical thinking, and character development in students, empowering them to achieve their full potential.

1965

Our school was established by Missionary Morana in 1965 and is among the oldest schools in Kenya

1982

We expanded our facilities to include science laboratories and a comprehensive library, enhancing our commitment to quality education.

1998

Our school was featured in the UNICEF education program to provide education for all.

2010

Our school became the best in the country and has never fallen from the 10 best ever since.

Our Mission

To educate and empower students with knowledge that will not only be useful in the career choices but also to make them ready to face life.

Our Vision

To create disciplined, well mannered and talented students who will use their knowledge to make the world a better place.
