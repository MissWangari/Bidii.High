How to Apply

01

Obtain Application Form

Visit the school office or download the admission form from our website. Fill out all required sections completely and accurately with the student's information.

02

Submit Required Documents

Provide certified copies of KCPE results, birth certificate, primary school leaving certificate, and passport-sized photographs as specified in the requirements.

03

Entrance Assessment

Selected candidates will sit for an entrance examination and participate in an interview with the admissions panel to assess academic readiness and fit.

04

Admission Confirmation

Successful applicants will receive an admission letter. Parents must confirm acceptance by paying the admission fee and returning the signed acceptance form within the stipulated time.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed Application Form
  • Original KCPE Certificate and Result Slip
  • Certified Copy of Birth Certificate
  • Primary School Leaving Certificate
  • Four Recent Passport-Size Photographs
  • Application Fee (KES 2,000 non-refundable)
  • Medical Examination Report from Certified Doctor

Transfer students from other secondary schools must provide a transfer letter from their previous school and academic transcripts for all completed terms.

School Fees Structure

Fee Category Amount (KES) Term
Tuition Fee 45,000 Per Term
Boarding Fee 30,000 Per Term
Day Scholar Fee 35,000 Per Term
Admission Fee 15,000 One-time

Bursaries & Scholarships Available

We offer merit-based scholarships and need-based bursaries to deserving students. Academic excellence awards cover up to 50% of tuition fees.
