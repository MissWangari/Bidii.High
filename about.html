Our Story

Educating Minds, Inspiring Hearts

Bidii High School was founded on the principles of academic excellence, moral integrity, and holistic development. Since our establishment, we have been dedicated to nurturing young minds and shaping future leaders who will make a positive impact on Kenya and the world. Our commitment to quality education has made us one of the most respected institutions in the country.

1965

Bidii High School was established by Missionary Morana with just 50 students and 5 teachers, becoming one of Kenya's pioneering secondary schools dedicated to academic excellence and character development.

1982

We expanded our facilities to include modern science laboratories, a comprehensive library with over 10,000 books, and expanded our capacity to serve 800 students, marking a new era of growth and innovation.

1998

Bidii High was selected to participate in the UNICEF Education for All program, receiving international recognition for our commitment to inclusive, quality education and community engagement.

2010

Our school achieved top 10 national ranking in KCSE examinations and has maintained this standard of excellence ever since, producing scholars, leaders, and innovators who excel locally and globally.

Our Mission

To provide quality, holistic education that develops academic excellence, strong moral character, and practical life skills, preparing students to become responsible citizens and future leaders who contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

To be the leading center of educational excellence in Kenya, recognized for nurturing disciplined, innovative, and globally competitive students who embody integrity, creativity, and a commitment to service.

Core Values

Academic Excellence

We pursue the highest standards in education, encouraging critical thinking, creativity, and a love for lifelong learning among our students.

Community Engagement

We actively participate in community service, environmental conservation, and social initiatives that create positive change in our society.

Innovation

We embrace new ideas, technologies, and teaching methods to prepare students for the challenges and opportunities of the modern world.

Global Perspective

We cultivate international awareness and cultural understanding through exchange programs, global partnerships, and exposure to diverse perspectives.
