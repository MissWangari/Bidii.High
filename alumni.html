Our Alumni Network

Since 1965, Bidii High School has produced outstanding graduates who have gone on to excel in diverse fields across Kenya and around the world. Our alumni community is a testament to the quality education and values instilled during their time at Bidii High. We are proud of their achievements and their continued commitment to excellence and service.

15k+ Alumni Worldwide
45+ Countries
60+ Years of Excellence

Success Stories

Meet some of our distinguished alumni making a difference in Kenya and beyond

Dr. James Mwangi

Class of 2005

Consultant Surgeon, Kenyatta National Hospital

Dr. Mwangi credits Bidii High for instilling the discipline and academic foundation that led him to medical school. He now serves as a leading surgeon and trains the next generation of Kenyan doctors.

Prof. David Omondi
